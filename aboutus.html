ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a travel agency that offers our clients custom and unforgettable travel experiences in the Caribbean. Our mission is to plan the perfect vacation for you! We can arrange everything you need to experience the greatest vacation of your life.

OUR BEGINNINGS

The company was founded in 2000, by Micky Danvers and her family in New York. It all started because the Davers desired to make a family trip to the Caribbean, and couldn’t find an angency that covered every aspect of the journey. Micky decided then to found an agency that could be able to solve everythig a client could possible dream of. That’s when they started to run the company in thei own apartment.

OUR PRESENT

Nowadays, our company remains as a family bussiness. The enterprise is run by Micky Danvers’s oldest daughter, Aria Danvers, and her youngest daughter, Leia Danvers, is in charge of the Marketing Department. Today, the company has more than 5.000 employees. They are the crucial part of the company, the ones that give the experience and warmth to our clients. We couldn’t be more proud of them!

OUR FUTURE

Currently we are developing our infraestructre to add about 2000 more employees, in order to expand our bussiness to include more destinations, hotels, and activities in our offers. Our goal is to finish the project by the end of 2022, and announce our new features on 2023. Keep in touch with us so you can be aware of what is coming next!
